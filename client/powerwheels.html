Power Wheels Racing

In association with Too Flat Scooter Club, several members of the LSDG are organizing a 'power wheels racing' event. Loosely based on the rules here: https://www.powerracingseries.org/rules, we will be modifying and hacking a Power Wheels styled children's ride-on toy for the purposes of racing by an adult.

This is just for fun, and the point is less the actual race, and more seeing what crazy things we can do to a child's toy. Still, we have a couple of guidelines:

  • No more than $500 can be spent in total. Honestly, we don't want to even get near this amount; it's all about creative reuse, repurposing, and recycling!
  • Voltages between 12v and 36v. This will help keeps things a bit safer.
  • Driver must be an adult. That's part of the whole point!
  • Be Safe. (Or as safe as one can be, given the premise.)

There are no entry fees, this is more for fun than anything else. Any additional inquiries should be sent to: Rusty Eulberg <festernd@gmail.com>
